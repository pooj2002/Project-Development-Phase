Services

Personal Expense Tracker application is a expense tracking system. It provides many usecases as it helps people to keep track of their spending habits.

Visualization

Expenses are visualized into bar graphs and pie-charts to create more insight into your spending habits.

Expense DB

Expenses can be added, edited and viewed. Expenses can also be included in different categories to track spending habits in each.

Email Alerts

Email Alerts are sent to the respective users' email when they exceed their budget limit. Budget limits can be modified to the users' need.

Download Reports

Reports can be generated for each user either weekly or monthly. Generated Reports can be either downloaded manually or sent through email.
